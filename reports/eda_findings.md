# Day 3 - EDA Findings

1. Investors aged 26–35 form the largest investor segment, accounting for 41.1% of all transactions.

2. Investors aged 36–45 represent the second-largest segment at 24.9%, indicating strong participation from working professionals.

3. SBI Mutual Fund has the highest Assets Under Management (AUM) among the analyzed fund houses.

4. ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund and HDFC Mutual Fund are the next largest AMCs by AUM, indicating concentration among a few major players.

5. Liquid Funds consistently attract the highest net inflows across the observed period.

6. Flexi Cap funds show stronger inflow trends compared to Large Cap funds.

7. T30 cities contribute 66.3% of mutual fund transactions, while B30 cities contribute 33.7%.

8. Mutual fund participation remains concentrated in major urban centers.

9. The correlation matrix suggests most schemes exhibit weak-to-moderate correlations rather than extremely strong relationships.

10. AUM in the mutual fund industry is concentrated among a relatively small number of large fund houses.
