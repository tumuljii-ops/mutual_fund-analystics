# Day 4 - Performance Analytics Findings

1. SBI Small Cap Fund (Regular Plan) delivered the strongest cumulative growth among the selected top-performing funds.

2. NIFTY MIDCAP 150 emerged as the best-performing benchmark index over the analysis period.

3. Small-cap focused funds and indices significantly outperformed large-cap benchmarks.

4. Debt-oriented benchmarks such as CRISIL Liquid and CRISIL Gilt exhibited stable but lower returns compared to equity benchmarks.

5. Fund performance varied considerably across categories, highlighting the importance of risk-adjusted fund selection.

6. The generated fund scorecard successfully ranked schemes using a composite methodology based on returns, Sharpe ratio, Alpha, expense ratio and drawdown metrics.

7. Alpha and Beta metrics were extracted and compiled for all schemes in the universe.

8. Benchmark comparison demonstrates that several actively managed funds outperformed major benchmark indices over the observed period.
